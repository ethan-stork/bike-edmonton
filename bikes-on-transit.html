Bikes On Transit

Did you know that, in part thanks to Bike Edmonton, all city public transit supports bikes in various ways?

Bikes On Buses

As of 2014, all full-size and articulated buses are equipped with bike racks. Previously, bike racks had only been available on select routes (beginning in 1996, thanks to Bike Edmontonâ€™s grant-funded purchase and donation of several racks).

Bikes On The LRT

Since 2021, you can bring your bike on the LRT at any time of day. When you do, please be mindful of those around you and try to not take up too much space.

Bike Parking At Facilities

ETS has secure indoor bike parking at Century Park Transit Centre. Access is 24/7 via scan card (available with a $10 deposit). You can find more information on the ETS website here. ETS also has bicycle storage lockers at South Campus/Fort Edmonton Park and Leger transit centres, as well as unsecured, outdoor racks at many other transit centres. Other municipalities around Edmonton also tend to have lockers, but please check with them accordingly.
