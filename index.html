About Bike Edmonton

Bike Edmonton was founded in 1980, under the name “Edmonton Bicycle Commuters Society,” as a non-profit organization. Ever since then, Bike Edmonton has been improving bikers daily experience with tools, bikes, volunteer service, and public lobbying.
